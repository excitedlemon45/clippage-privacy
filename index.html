ClipPage Pro Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

TL;DR: ClipPage Pro does NOT collect any data. Everything stays on your device. Your privacy is 100% protected. Completely free forever.

Overview

ClipPage Pro is committed to protecting your privacy. This Chrome extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data whatsoever. All features are completely free with no limitations.

Data Collection

ClipPage Pro does NOT collect:

  • Browsing history
  • Personal information
  • Screenshot content or copied text
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Cookies or tracking data
  • Any information about you or your activities

How ClipPage Pro Works

All screenshot processing and text extraction happens locally on your device:

  • Screenshots are generated on your computer
  • Text recognition (OCR) is processed locally
  • Files are saved directly to your Downloads folder
  • Copied text goes to your clipboard only
  • Nothing is uploaded to any server
  • Nothing is transmitted over the internet
  • Your data never leaves your device

Permissions Explained

ClipPage Pro requests the following permissions to function:

  • activeTab: To capture the current webpage you're viewing
  • scripting: To inject screenshot capture and text extraction code into webpages
  • downloads: To save screenshots to your Downloads folder
  • storage: To save your preferences (like default capture mode, delay settings) locally on your device
  • clipboardWrite: To copy extracted text to your clipboard

These permissions are used ONLY for the core functionality of taking screenshots and extracting text. No data is collected or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

ClipPage Pro does not use:

  • Analytics services (no Google Analytics, no tracking)
  • Advertising networks
  • Data collection tools
  • External servers or APIs
  • Cloud storage services
