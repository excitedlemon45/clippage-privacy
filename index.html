ClipPage Pro Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

TL;DR: ClipPage Pro does NOT collect any data. Everything stays on your device. Your privacy is 100% protected.

Overview

ClipPage Pro is committed to protecting your privacy. This Chrome extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data whatsoever.

Data Collection

ClipPage Pro does NOT collect:

  • Browsing history
  • Personal information
  • Screenshot content
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Cookies or tracking data
  • Any information about you or your activities

How ClipPage Pro Works

All screenshot processing happens locally on your device:

  • Screenshots are generated on your computer
  • Files are saved directly to your Downloads folder
  • Nothing is uploaded to any server
  • Nothing is transmitted over the internet
  • Your data never leaves your device

Permissions Explained

ClipPage Pro requests the following permissions to function:

  • activeTab: To capture the current webpage you're viewing
  • scripting: To inject screenshot capture code into webpages
  • downloads: To save screenshots to your Downloads folder
  • storage: To save your preferences (like default capture mode) locally on your device

These permissions are used ONLY for the core functionality of taking screenshots. No data is collected or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

ClipPage Pro does not use:

  • Analytics services (no Google Analytics, no tracking)
  • Advertising networks
  • Data collection tools
  • External servers or APIs

Payment Processing (Pro Version)

If you choose to upgrade to ClipPage Pro Premium, payment processing is handled securely by Stripe. We do not store or have access to your payment information. Please see Stripe's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle payment data.

Children's Privacy

ClipPage Pro does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The extension is safe for users of all ages.
